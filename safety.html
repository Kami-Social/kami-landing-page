Safety Standards

Kami is committed to maintaining a safe environment for users and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material, child sexual exploitation, grooming, or any activity that endangers minors.

Users may report safety concerns, abusive behavior, or suspected child exploitation by emailing hello@kamisocial.com.

Kami reviews safety reports and may remove content, restrict accounts, suspend users, or take other appropriate action when behavior violates our policies or applicable law.

Kami complies with applicable child safety laws and will report child sexual abuse material or exploitation concerns to appropriate regional or national authorities where required.

Kami is not directed to children under 13. Users must meet applicable age requirements to use the app.

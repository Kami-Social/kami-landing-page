A note on privacy

Kami uses cloud data to support real-time nearby discovery. Kami is designed around real-life discovery rather than long-term location tracking, and we aim to minimize unnecessary location retention. Read our full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Kami?

Kami is a real-world discovery app that helps you find nearby people, places, and events worth showing up for.

Why does Kami ask for my Instagram?

Kami uses your Instagram handle to understand lightweight public social context, so nearby people feel less random and easier to connect with.

How does Kami know who’s around me?

Kami uses location permission to understand nearby presence and discovery context. The goal is to help you see what is nearby without turning the app into a permanent location-tracking feed.

What happens when there is someone worth meeting?

Kami may notify you when someone nearby seems relevant or interesting. You can also browse the app directly to discover nearby people, places, and events.

Is my data secure?

Kami uses cloud infrastructure because real-time discovery requires it. We keep the privacy promise narrow and honest: the product is designed around timely nearby context rather than long-term location tracking, and we aim to minimize unnecessary location retention.

How do I join the beta?

Kami beta is available on Android through Google Play and on iPhone through TestFlight. Visit the download section, enter your email, and follow the install link for your platform.

How TestFlight works

TestFlight is Apple’s official beta testing app. It lets iPhone users try apps before they are publicly available in the App Store.

  1. Install the TestFlight app from Apple.
  2. Open the Kami TestFlight invitation link.
  3. Tap Accept.
  4. Install Kami.
  5. Open the app and start exploring Austin in real life.
